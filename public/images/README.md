# Your photos go here

Drop your images in this folder:

- `portrait.jpg` (or .png/.webp) — main photo, ideally portrait orientation ~4:5 (e.g. 1200×1500).
  Then update `portraitSrc` in `src/components/site/About.tsx` from `/images/portrait.svg` to your file.

`portrait.svg` is the placeholder shown until you do.
